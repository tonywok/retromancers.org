Monthly Tournament

Occurs on the first Tuesday of each month. Event starts at 6:30 PM

Hosted at Warp Gate. $10 entry. Prizing will be store credit based on attendence.

The tourney is unsanctioned. Gold border and proxies are allowed. If proxying, please at least print an image of the card.

Monthly Casual Wednesday Friendly

Occurs on the third Wednesday of each month. Open play starts at 6 PM.

Hosted at Forge Tavern. You are expected to buy food or drink.
